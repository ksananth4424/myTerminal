usage: myps [option]
ps displays information about a selection of the active processes.

Options that myShell(myps) can perform are:
    1) -e Select all processes.
    2) -a Select all processes except both session leaders and processes not associated with a terminal.
    3) -F Extra full format.
    4) -aF Extra full format for processes displayed in (myps -a).
    5) -eF Extra full format for all processes.
